Brownies

Description

Brownies are the ultimate treat for anyone with a sweet tooth, striking that perfect balance between a dense, fudgy texture and a slight crumbliness on the edges. They’re one of those desserts that are super comforting, indulgent, and surprisingly versatile.

Imagine a rich, chocolatey base that’s almost like a cross between a cake and a cookie. When you bite into one, you get that deep, intense chocolate flavor that melts in your mouth, with a slightly chewy or fudgy center, depending on how they’re made. The outer edges are slightly crisp, giving you that satisfying contrast in texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar (for extra chewiness)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (good quality, for best flavor)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional, for extra chocolatey goodness)
