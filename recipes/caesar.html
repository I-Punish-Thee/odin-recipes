Caesar Salad

Description

Though often served as a side dish, a Caesar salad with protein can easily stand as a main course. It's known for its rich, savory, and tangy flavor profile.Though often served as a side dish, a Caesar salad with protein can easily stand as a main course. It's known for its rich, savory, and tangy flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce – 1 large head or about 6 cups, chopped
  • Croutons – 1 to 2 cups, homemade or store-bought
  • Parmesan cheese – 1/4 to 1/2 cup, freshly grated or shaved
  • Egg yolk – 1 (optional; some recipes use mayonnaise instead)
  • Garlic – 1 clove, minced
  • Anchovy fillets – 2 to 3, mashed (or 1 tsp anchovy paste)
  • Dijon mustard – 1 tsp
  • Worcestershire sauce – 1 tsp
  • Fresh lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Olive oil – 1/3 to 1/2 cup
  • Parmesan cheese – 2 tbsp, grated (adds body to the dressing)
  • Salt and black pepper – to taste
